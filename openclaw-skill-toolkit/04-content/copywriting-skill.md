# Copywriting Skill

Write marketing copy that converts — landing pages, product descriptions, ads.

## When to Use

Creating marketing materials. Landing pages, product pages, ad copy, email sequences, or any promotional content.

## The Skill

```
Write copy for:

TYPE: [landing page / product description / ad / email / social ad]
PRODUCT/OFFER: [what you're selling]
AUDIENCE: [who will see this]
GOAL: [sign up / purchase / click / etc.]
KEY BENEFIT: [the one thing that matters most]

CONTEXT:
- Product details: [what it does]
- Price/value: [if relevant]
- Differentiation: [why this vs alternatives]
- Proof: [testimonials, data, social proof available]

GENERATE:

## Headline Variants
[3 headline options]
- Pattern: [benefit-driven / question / bold claim]

## Subheadline
[One sentence expanding on the headline]

## Body Copy Structure
- Problem: [what's broken / frustrating]
- Agitation: [make the problem feel real]
- Solution: [how your product fixes it]
- Proof: [why you're credible]

## Call to Action
[Button/link text, make it specific not generic]

## Supporting Points
[3 bullet points covering secondary benefits]

FORMAT OPTIONS:

For LANDING PAGE:
Follow: Headline → Subheadline → Hero image description → Problem → Solution → Features → Social proof → CTA

For PRODUCT DESCRIPTION:
Follow: Hook → Features → Benefits → Proof → CTA

For AD COPY:
Follow: Attention → Interest → Desire → Action
```

## Example

```
TYPE: Landing page
PRODUCT: Project management tool for small teams
AUDIENCE: 5-20 person agencies
GOAL: Free trial signups
KEY BENEFIT: Stop wasting time in status meetings

OUTPUT:

HEADLINES:
1. Your team doesn't need another meeting
2. Status updates without the status meeting
3. Know what everyone's working on without asking

SUBHEADLINE:
[Product name] shows you progress, blockers, and priorities in one view — no more hour-long standups.

PROBLEM:
Your team spends 5+ hours a week in meetings that could be emails. Half the info is outdated by the time you need it. Everyone's "busy" but nothing ships.

AGITATION:
Sound familiar? You're not alone. Agencies lose 12 hours per week on average to unnecessary sync meetings.

SOLUTION:
[Product] replaces daily standups with a 2-minute async update. Your team posts what they did, what they're doing next, and any blockers — by 10am. You see it all in one dashboard.

PROOF:
Used by 500+ agencies. "We cut meetings by 60%" — [client name]

CTA:
Start your free trial →

FEATURES:
- Async updates in under 2 minutes
- Auto-detect blockers
- Integrates with Slack, Notion, GitHub
```

## Tips

- Lead with benefit, not feature — "ship faster" beats "workflow automation"
- Specific beats vague — "3 hours saved" beats "more productive"
- One offer, one goal — don't ask for email AND purchase
- Test headlines — what sounds good isn't always what converts

## Variations

**Email subject lines:** Generate 10 variants with different angles
**Facebook ads:** Add "why now" urgency
**App store:** Focus on key differentiator, hit search terms
**Sales page:** Full long-form with objection handling
