# AI Prompt Templates Bundle

A collection of 20+ templates for getting more out of AI tools like ChatGPT and Claude.

## What You Get

A folder full of markdown files, each one a template for a different use case. Pick the one that matches what you're trying to do, fill in the brackets with your specific situation, and paste it into your AI tool of choice.

## Categories

**Writing Templates** - for when you need to churn out blog posts, emails, social content, video scripts, that sort of thing.

**Coding Templates** - code reviews, tracking down bugs, planning APIs, writing docs, generating tests.

**Marketing Templates** - SEO articles that might actually rank, competitor research, product descriptions that don't sound like every other product description.

**Research Templates** - market analysis, user interviews, keeping tabs on trends.

**Productivity Templates** - meeting notes that are actually useful, breaking down projects, making decisions without endless back-and-forth.

## Why These Exist

Most prompt templates you find online are either too vague to be useful or so long nobody actually reads them. These are meant to be practical. Copy, paste, tweak, go.

## How to Use

1. Look through the folders and find something that matches your need
2. Open that file and copy the template
3. Paste it into your AI chat
4. Replace anything in [brackets] with your actual info
5. Hit send

## Compatibility

Works with ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and basically any other LLM out there. The principles are the same regardless of which tool you're using.
